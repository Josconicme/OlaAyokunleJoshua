MindCare – Full-Stack Mental Health Wellness Platform

A comprehensive MERN stack application providing personalized mental health support with mood tracking, journaling, and evidence-based recovery programs.

CategoryWeb Application
StackMERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js)
FocusMental Health & Wellness

Project Overview

MindCare is a comprehensive mental health wellness platform designed to provide personalized support for users on their mental health journey. The application combines evidence-based therapeutic techniques with modern web technologies to create a compassionate and accessible user experience.

Key Features

  • Premium Patient Dashboard: Real-time wellness metrics including daily streaks, task completion rates, and "mindful minutes" tracking
  • Interactive Mood Tracking: Log emotional states, attach reflections, and visualize emotional patterns through historical data
  • Full-Stack Journaling: Secure, private journaling platform with complete CRUD functionality
  • Evidence-Based Recovery Programs: Structured programs for Anxiety and Depression with progress tracking
  • Automated Task Scheduling: Dynamic content delivery based on user progress and needs
  • Smooth Animations: Framer Motion powered transitions for compassionate UX
